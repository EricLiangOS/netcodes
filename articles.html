The Importance of Articles

From a Student's Perspective

The world of computer science is constantly shifting with new innovations being created every single day, so it's often difficult to keep up with the newest trends; while one student may be a savant in a particular topic, they could be clueless in a totally different aspect. However, with a common platform to share personal tips, experiences, and knowledge, students can become a master of all topics, all in a fashion understandable by high school students.

Our articles are specifically designed to help students interested in computer science by providing different types of articles, such as guides, information articles, and personal blogs. These articles are carefully edited and revised to be as educational as possible, with the sole purpose of educating students. You can even find the specific articles you want to read about by filtering the articles.

Want to Help Write One?

We are always looking for new talented writers to help expand our catalog of student resources. Writing articles are a great way to express your passion for computer science and enhance your own knowledge, while also contributing back to our netcodes community. Writing articles for netcodes counts as 4 hours of community service per article, and even shows dedication and commitment on your future college or job applications (You can link your articles on LinkedIn).

If you are interested in becoming a dedicated netcodes writer or just want to contribute a single article, you can fill out the application below! You can provide copies to any articles that you would like to be featured that are relevant to computer science, and we will look through and consider publishing it; check back regularly to see if your article has been accepted. The articles can be about any topic you like, as long as it pertains to computer science!
