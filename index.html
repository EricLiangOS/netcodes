Overview

An Internet Network for Students

Computer science is one of the fastest growing careers in modern times, with the number of CS jobs over doubling in the last decade. With such a large yet new industry, it's difficult to find a place to start learning about computer science, which is why netcodes is here to help.

At netcodes, we provide opportunities for students around the world in our free computer science network, dedicated to helping these students succeed. Students in netcodes are able to learn new things about computer science and improve their own skills, all in an engaging fashion.

Learn from the best with the best webinars

Computer science is an incredibly expansive industry with countless fields, meaning there's an equally large number of professionals that excel in their area of work. Learn from these professionals about their story and their work.

Every few weeks, we invite industry professionals to come and give presentations about their work, ranging from AI to biomedical engineering. In these webinars, students are able to ask questions and make connections with these experts to further their own computer science journey.

Hackathons Aren't Just for Hackers

We have always believed that doing something yourself is the best way to learn, and what better way to practice computer science then with hackathons?

In our monthly hackathons, brainstorm and implement a project from scratch revolving around that month's theme. Work by yourself or with others in order to create a finished product that contributes back to the community, all with a chance to earn amazing prizes.

Mission

Mission Statement

The computers that were once limited to government headquarters are now in the pockets and hands of most students around the globe, providing a whole new world of opportunities for students. At netcodes, we want to help students interested in computer science attain their full potential with our events and opportunities. Even though many resources exist online for those interesting in coding, netcodes wants to provide teenagers with firsthand anecdotes and opportunities to utilize computer science, raising interest and expertise in the field, planting the seeds for a skill that will grow to help humanity.
