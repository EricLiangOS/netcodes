WiFi to the World

Overview

Equal Access to WiFi For All

The internet is a hub of education, shopping, entertainment, and connection, yet over a million people in the United States alone don't have access to this resource. This leaves people like the homeless at a sever disadvantage in their daily lives, making it even harder to escape poverty.

At netcodes, we are determined to combat this inequality in technology, called the digital divide, by raising money through tutoring and using this money to provide internet to homeless shelters. We hope you can join us in our cause to end the digital divide by donating or contributing to our cause.

What Inspires Us?

The internet is something that a majority of people are fortunate enough to take for granted, leaving us to forget just how crucial it is for our daily lives. After realizing that there are many people who don't have access to the internet, we were motivated to cover this digital divide and provide this resource for those who need it.

We understand that no matter how much money we raise to aid those without internet, there will still be more people for us to help. That is why we move forward in our goal without stopping or giving up, up until everyone has access to the internet.

Why Tutoring?

Providing WiFi comes at a cost, so we decide to use our skills of teaching and leadership for tutoring to raise money. We are passionate in helping others by both providing internet and helping students grow at the same time.

In addition to being proficient in many common areas of school, including science, math, and English, we have prior experience in tutoring and making sure students understand their course material to help them succeed in school.

Mission Statement

One modern resource that many people rely on is the internet, which has improved our quality of life to indescribable measures, but it also leaves those without internet at a significant disadvantage. At netcodes, we are dedicated to ending this digital divide by providing internet for socioeconmically disadvantaged people who don't have access to the internet, including homeless shelters and impoverished families. We envision a world where people regardless of their background can use the shared tools of the internet, not limited by their financial status or having to worry about paying the internet bill. We will work towards our goal with fundraising one wifi signal at a time.
