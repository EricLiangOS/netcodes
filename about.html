About Us

A team of high school students with a vision

Who are we?

Our Background

We're a group of high school students trying to improve the world in this digital day and age. Although we are fortunate enough to utilize technology freely, we realize that not everyone has the same luxury as us, so we hope to bring this crucial resource into more people's lives. The netcodes team is not only dedicated to providing internet access to those who need it most, but we also enjoy teaching others through tutoring. netcodes combines our passion and our mission to improve the world.

Our Team

Eric Liang

Starting his junior year this year, Eric Liang is a high school student who is passionate about computer science and improving the quality of life using technology. He enjoys using computer science to find the solution to solve problems, and enjoys hiking in his free time.

Eric realized the importance of the internet after volunteering at a local food drive, where he realized many of the people there who needed food didn't have the resources to search for food drives online and relied only on word of mouth.

Nayan Ramam

Nayan Ramam is a junior in high school who loves math and engineering. Outside of school he likes to play basketball, produce music, and solve Rubik's cubes competitively.

The digital divide has always been an important part of the STEM lessons that Nayan's learned from school, but the actual statistics on how little access homeless people had to technology both shocked and motivated Nayan to do something that can change these statistics.

What Inspires Us?

The internet is something that a majority of people are fortunate enough to take for granted, leaving us to forget just how crucial it is for our daily lives. After realizing that there are many people who don't have access to the internet, we were motivated to cover this digital divide and provide this resource for those who need it.

We understand that no matter how much money we raise to aid those without internet, there will still be more people for us to help. That is why we move forward in our goal without stopping or giving up, up until everyone has access to the internet.

About

netcodes is a nonprofit organization dedicated to connecting the digitally disadvantaged people of the world with internet, one of the most important resources of the 21st century, by fundraising through activities like tutoring!
