About Us

A team of high school students with a vision

Who Are We?

We're a group of high school students trying to improve the world in this digital day and age. Although we are fortunate enough to utilize technology freely, we realize that not everyone has the same luxury as us, so we hope to bring this crucial resource into more people's lives. The netcodes team is not only dedicated to providing internet access to those who need it most, but we also enjoy teaching others through tutoring. netcodes combines our passion and our mission to improve the world.

About

netcodes is a nonprofit organization dedicated to connecting the digitally disadvantaged people of the world with internet, one of the most important resources of the 21st century, by fundraising through activities like tutoring!
