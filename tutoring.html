100% Non-profit

As part of our mission to reduce the technological equity gap and provide internet access to more people, 100% of our proceeds will go to servicing internet connections for homeless shelters and families in need.

We search for local homeless shelters in San Jose that currently do not provide their residents with internet and work with internet service companies in order to pay for their internet bills every month. Any excess funds raised will go towards providing additional internet service for other homeless shelters.

Affordable Tutoring Prices

Although we all our proceeds go to providing homeless shelters with internet connections, we are still committed to providing a high-quality tutoring service at an affordable price. That's why our tutoring costs will always remain at $5 an hour, no matter how many hours you need help with.

To ensure the quality of our tutoring services, your first hour of tutoring is free with us. If our tutoring style doesn't exactly suit you, this is the perfect way to test it out.

Our Tutoring

If you or anyone you know needs help with any academic subjects, ranging from teest preparation and homework to outside competitions, we would love to help you out! Our tutors are experienced, high-achieving students that are familiar with many academic concepts as well as being able to effectively tutor and communicate with students.

We are also happy to accommodate to our tutee's needs based on location and time. We'd love to work with you to determine the tutoring style that works best, whether after-school in libraries or weekend mornings online. Contact us for more detail!

Pricing

We charge for an hourly rate of $5.00 for our tutoring services, preferrably through cash. However, our first meeting is always free so you or your student can get a feel of how our tutoring works and you can join if you're interested.
