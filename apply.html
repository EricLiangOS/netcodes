Apply

Join a team dedicated to empowering computer science students

We're Currently Accepting New Team Members!

There's plenty of reasons for applying to netcodes!

1. Gain Experience

Joining an organization is the ideal position for a high school student because it comes with so many perks! If you have ever been in an organization before, you know the fun of meeting new people and organizing exciting events; if not, this is your chance to find out!

The netcodes team is the perfect environment for self-growth, allowing you to learn more about computer science and improving your communication and organization skills. netcodes also gives sought-after experience that will inevitably come in useful in the future because of its prestige, whether for college admissions or job applications.

2. Meet New People

The netcodes team is a vast and diverse group of people all bonded by the beuaty of computer science, so you're sure to meet someone that shares similar interests here. By working closely together with people, you have the chance to see the world of computer science through new lenses and get help in learning about new topics.

Not only can you bond with peers also on netcodes, but you can get exclusive access to meeting industry professionals. Through our webinars and hackathons, you get to work closely with companies and professional software engineers, which can help you network and learn advanced concepts.

3. Get Volunteer Hours

Volunteer hours is something that most people dread getting, whether for school, clubs, or other organizations. However, working at netcodes is a unique experience that can help with your volunteering endeavors.

Team members will get volunteer hours based on the amount of work they accomplish, which are certified. Volunteering not only contributes back to your community and improves the lives of other students, but can also make yourself eligible for awards like the Presidential Volunteer Service Award.

The Application Process

Do any of the reasons listed above sound interesting to you? If so, you should definitely apply for one of the positions avaliable at netcodes! We are looking for individuals who not only have a passion for computer science, but are also willing to put in the effort to improve computer science education for others. Positions range from journalists to being on our board of directors, all of which will greatly benefit the experiences of netcodes students.

After filling out the application form below, we will carefully review your application and notify you of your application status once we are done. Some of the positions may have multiple stages of applications, such as interviews (These are listed in the descriptions down below). For journalism team applicants, please apply through the form in the Articles page

Good luck to all our applicants!

Avaliable Role Descriptions

Marketing Director*

Description

Part of netcodes's mission is to spread computer science opportunities to students around the world and providing them with opportunities, but this can only happen if they know what netcodes is. The marketing director is in charge of connecting with students and outlining to them what netcodes is and how it can benefit them in order to encourage more students to attend our events. The marketing director will lead the marketing team in contacting schools and students, as well as oversee our social media accounts.

Requirements

  • - Prior leadership experience
  • - Communication skills
  • - Organization skills
  • - Advertisement/marketing experience
  • - Contribute at least one article a month
  • - 2 to 3 hours a week

Who Should Apply?

This role is ideal for students that are adept at marketing and spreading awareness about our organization. This student should be comfortable with mass emailing schools and getting their students interested in netcodes.

*This role requires an additional interview after the application

Outreach Director*

Description

Many of netcodes's opportunities relies on contacting experienced programmers and computer scientists to give deliver webinars and companies to sponsor hackathons. This is why being the outreach director is a critical role at netcodes, tasked with finding speakers and sponsors. This position comes with the benefit of networking with these companies/professionals and gaining expereince for communicating with others.

Requirements

  • - Communications skills
  • - Proactive and persistent in emails
  • - Persuasive (Demonstrate why sponsoring/speaking is a good idea)
  • - Contribute at least one article a month
  • - 3 to 4 hours a week

Who Should Apply?

Students who want to improve their communications skill should apply for this role as they can learn how to effectively communicate and work with others. Those who are persuasive should also apply as it is an important part of getting access to more opportunities.

*This role requires an additional interview after the application

Programs Director*

Description

At netcodes, we have a plethora of opportunities for students to partake in. Part of the program director's job is to organize these events to appeal to students and make it a enriching experience. This involves coming up with new ideas for hackathons and finding interesting topics to cover in webinars. The programs director will work with the outreach director to make these opportunities possible, as well as partially oversee the journalism team.

Requirements

  • - Prior leadership experience
  • - Creativity
  • - Organization skills
  • - Flexible
  • - Contribute at least one article a month
  • - 3 to 4 hours a week

Who Should Apply?

For students who love organizing exciting events and bringing them into fruition, this role is perfect for you. This position gives students experience in making a large impact by allowing them to provide educational opportunities for netcodes.

*This role requires an additional interview after the application

Journalism Team

Description

There are countless topics in computer science that are unnoticed by students around the world, which is why netcodes seeks to bring these subjects into light. The journalism team is responsible for increasing interest in computer science by writing about personal interests.

Requirements

  • - Passion for writing and computer science
  • - Contribute at least three articles a month
  • - 1 to 2 hours a week

Who Should Apply?

Many students enjoy creative writing, and this role gives these students to express their passion for computer science. Rather than being limited to a specific essay prompt, netcodes journalists can cover whatever topic they enjoy spread it to others.

See the Articles section to learn more about this position.

Marketing Team

Part of netcodes's mission is to spread computer science opportunities to students around the world and providing them with opportunities, but this can only happen if they know what netcodes is. Members of the marketing team are responsible for attracting new students to netcodes and introducing them to the opportunities that we provide here. Certain members will also manage and create content for our social media.
